CAMERON BRAY


Since 2018 I have been studying an MA in Architecture at Central Saint Martins in London.

The work from this course that you can see on this website broadly falls into two pieces of work.
Both investigate the same corner of South East London - the area around Surrey Canal Road

Here you can find my study of an industrial estate under threat of redevelopment,and the stories of those living and working there.

Here you can find a project investigating a waste-to-energy incineration plant nearby. I imagine its reincarnation as a Civic Waste Facility.
