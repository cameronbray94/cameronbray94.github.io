CAMERON BRAY
bio


Hi! I’m Cameron. I work across architecture, art, radio and performance.

I grew up in New Delhi, Cumbria and Scotland, and now I live in London. I studied architecture in Edinburgh and then at Central Saint Martins in London, where my work focused on waste.

I’m interested in talking and listening, as well as the collision points between the global and the local.
Big things and small things.

I work as at JA Projects, at CSM MArch, as well as with public works at R-Urban Poplar. I host a monthly radio show on Radio Alhara and run regular cycle tours of the Grand Surrey Canal with Open City.

In 2016, I co-founded a collective called Civic Soup. In the past I’ve worked with Recetas Urbanas in Madrid, studio umschichten in Stuttgart and Urbz in Mumbai.
