CAMERON BRAY
bio


hi I’m Cameron. I like to make art, architecture, performances, sound pieces and also food.
I grew up in New Delhi and in Cumbria, now I live in London. I have recently finished a Masters in Architecture at Central Saint Martins, where my work has focused on waste.
I’m interested in the collision point between the global and the local. Big things and small things.
I'm part of a collective called Civic Soup, founded in Edinburgh in 2016.
At the moment I'm doing some work with public works and JA Projects. I also run regular tours of the Grand Surrey Canal.
I'm working on some independent projects of my own, and I'm hoping to have my first radio show soon too! In the past I’ve worked with Recetas Urbanas in Madrid, studio umschichten in Stuttgart, Urbz in Mumbai and also Richard Taylor, in Morden.
I hope you enjoy your time on my website, please don’t hesitate to get in touch!
