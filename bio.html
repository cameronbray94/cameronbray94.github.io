CAMERON BRAY
bio


Hi! I’m Cameron. I like to make architecture, art, performances, sound pieces and also food.

I grew up in New Delhi, Cumbria and Scotland, and now I live in London. I recently finished a Masters in Architecture at Central Saint Martins, where my work has focused on waste.

I’m interested in the collision point between the global and the local.
Big things and small things.

I'm part of a collective called Civic Soup, founded in Edinburgh in 2016. At the moment I'm doing some work with public works and JA Projects. Occasionally I work with OfCA. I also run regular tours of the Grand Surrey Canal.

In the past I’ve worked with Recetas Urbanas in Madrid, studio umschichten in Stuttgart and Urbz in Mumbai.

I hope you enjoy your time on my website, please don’t hesitate to get in touch!
