Car Door Sound System, 2019

A working speaker set-up using a car door donated by Dominic and Darek from London Remaps.
I added a surface transducer, so that the door itself vibrated to produce sound.
The steel frame was constructed at CSM and is designed as a trolley for portability. I took it on the tube once.
I hope to build more units to set up a genuine car door sound system as a future installation piece.
